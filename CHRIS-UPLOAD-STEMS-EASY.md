## TROUBLESHOOTING:

**"Upload failed"**
- Make sure you're signed into Google Drive (green checkmark at top)
- Make sure your internet connection is good
- Try refreshing the page and uploading again

**"Files uploading slowly"**
- Large files take time (5-10 MB files might take 30-60 seconds each)
- Don't close the browser while uploading
- Progress bar shows you which file is uploading

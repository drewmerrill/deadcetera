## Details

### Pre-Sync: "Creating your playlist…"
Fires immediately on tap. Signals intent and ownership — "your playlist" not "syncing data."

### Playlist Identity: "{Type} playlist ready — N songs in order"
Uses human bundle name (Gig/Rehearsal/Focus/North Star). Confirms count and order. Shows for 400ms before opening Spotify — enough to read, not enough to slow down.

### Completion: "All songs fixed — your playlist is complete"
After re-sync when all previously failed songs are now matched. Stronger signal than "improved" — communicates finality and completeness.

## Edge Cases

- **Partial fix on re-sync:** Shows "2 songs fixed — playlist improved" (not "complete")
- **No improvement on re-sync:** No extra toast (only standard sync messages)
- **Already up to date:** "Already up to date — opening Spotify" (unchanged)

## Success Confirmation

When a source resolves and playback starts:

```
✔ Playing: Scarlet Begonias · YouTube · Best available version
```

The source label animates in with a fade. Song title in bold. Source and confidence as secondary context.

## Now Playing + Next Up

Progress bar now shows: **"Now Playing · 3 of 12"** (not just "3 of 12").

Up next shows: **"Next up: Fire on the Mountain"** or **"🎶 Last song in the set"**.

Persistent context — user always knows where they are in the queue.

## Fallback UX (Choice, Not Failure)

When all sources fail, the video container shows:

> **Couldn't find a perfect match**
> Choose how to listen:

Below that:
- **Try Again** + **Next Song** (side by side, equal weight)
- **Open in YouTube** + **Open in Spotify** (platform links, secondary)
- **"Have a link? Paste it here"** (collapsible)

Framed as a choice, not a failure. No "Retry" as primary action.

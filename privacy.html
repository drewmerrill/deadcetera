Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08 · Operator: GrooveLinx (Andrew Merrill, sole proprietor) · Contact: support@groovelinx.com

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how GrooveLinx ("we", "us", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information about its users. The Service is a private, invitation-based web application at groovelinx.com used by music bands to coordinate rehearsals, gigs, setlists, and member availability. We do not market the Service to the general public.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and an optional profile photo, supplied by the user when they create an account or sign in via Google.
  • Phone number: only if the user voluntarily provides one for SMS notifications. Storage is opt-in; the field is empty by default.
  • Band activity: rehearsal/gig schedules, setlists, song notes, availability blocks, RSVPs, polls — all created by users about their own band.
  • Calendar metadata: for users who connect Google Calendar, GrooveLinx reads and writes events on a user-designated band calendar to keep schedules synchronized. We do not access events on the user's other personal calendars beyond the configured one(s).
  • Technical logs: standard server logs (IP address, user-agent, request paths, timestamps) retained for operational debugging and abuse prevention.

How we use information

  • Operating the Service: rendering each user's view of their own band's data.
  • Sending notifications the user has opted in to (in-app banners, browser push, SMS).
  • Synchronizing schedules with the user's connected Google Calendar at their request.
  • Diagnosing errors and abuse.

We do not sell user data. We do not share user data with advertisers. We do not use user data to train machine-learning models for anyone other than the user's own band's intelligence features (e.g., rehearsal recommendations).

SMS notifications specifically

  • SMS is opt-in; the setting is OFF by default in every user's account.
  • Users opt in by entering their phone number in Settings → Notifications, in the SMS Notifications section, and explicitly clicking Enable.
  • Phone numbers are used only to send notifications to the user about activity in their own band.
  • Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS or by clicking Disable on the SMS toggle in the app.
  • SMS delivery is performed by Twilio (twilio.com); their handling of the underlying telecom data is governed by Twilio's own privacy policy.

Mobile information and SMS opt-in consent data are not shared with third parties. No mobile information, phone numbers, or SMS opt-in consent data collected by GrooveLinx will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of information described in this Privacy Policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information is not shared with any third parties under any circumstance.

Service providers

GrooveLinx uses the following processors to operate:

  • Firebase (Google Cloud) — application data storage and authentication.
  • Google Cloud Identity / OAuth — sign-in via Google.
  • Google Calendar API — calendar synchronization at user request.
  • Twilio — SMS delivery for users who opted in.
  • Cloudflare — proxy/edge for some API endpoints.
  • Vercel — static asset hosting.

Data retention

Account data is retained while the account is active. Users can delete their account by contacting support@groovelinx.com; account data is removed within 30 days of request. Operational logs are retained up to 90 days.

Your rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@groovelinx.com. You may opt out of SMS notifications at any time via the Service settings or by replying STOP. You may opt out of all GrooveLinx data collection by deleting your account.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification. Continued use of the Service after a change constitutes acceptance.

📋 Your Practice Workflow:

✅ STEP 1: Download MP3

Click "Download MP3" button above → Find the file on Archive.org → Right-click → Save to Downloads

⬆️ STEP 2: Upload to Moises

Click "Open Moises.ai Studio" → Click Upload → Select your downloaded MP3

⚙️ STEP 3: Separate Stems

Choose "6 Stems" separation → Wait 1-2 minutes for AI processing

🎸 STEP 4: Solo Your Part

Click "Solo" on your instrument → Adjust tempo → Loop sections → Practice!

GrooveLinx SMS Notifications

How members of bands using GrooveLinx opt in to and out of SMS notifications about their band's schedule.

Operator: GrooveLinx (Andrew Merrill, sole proprietor) · Last updated 2026-05-12 · support@groovelinx.com

Program at a glance

  • Program name: GrooveLinx Band Notifications
  • Operator: GrooveLinx (Andrew Merrill, sole proprietor)
  • Recipients: Members of the operator's own band(s) who have explicitly opted in inside the GrooveLinx app
  • Content: Rehearsal time changes, soundcheck reminders, setlist updates, gig confirmations, and band schedule changes
  • Frequency: Message frequency varies, typically 2–5 messages per week.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply. GrooveLinx does not charge.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any GrooveLinx SMS, or disable SMS in Settings → Notifications.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any GrooveLinx SMS, or email support@groovelinx.com.

Who receives messages

Only people who are:

  1. Personally invited by their band leader to join GrooveLinx,
  2. Have created an account at app.groovelinx.com using their Google account,
  3. Have signed in to that account, and
  4. Have explicitly enabled SMS notifications themselves in their account settings (the option is OFF by default — users must explicitly click Enable SMS).

Each band typically has 3–7 members. There is no marketing list, no purchased data, no third-party data sourcing, and no outreach to anyone outside an existing band.

How to opt in (step by step)

  1. Sign in to your account at app.groovelinx.com using your Google account.
  2. Open the menu and select Settings.
  3. Click the Notifications tab inside Settings.
  4. Scroll down to the 📱 SMS Notifications section.
  5. Enter your mobile phone number in the phone field.
  6. Click the Enable SMS button. (The button is OFF by default — you must explicitly click Enable SMS.)

Immediately after you click Enable SMS, GrooveLinx sends you a confirmation SMS.

Disclosure shown above the Enable SMS button (verbatim)

The following disclosure text is displayed inside the GrooveLinx app, immediately above the Enable SMS button on the SMS Notifications opt-in form. By clicking Enable SMS, the user is providing express written consent to receive the messages described:

By enabling, you authorize GrooveLinx to send SMS to this number about rehearsal schedules, gigs, setlist updates, and time-sensitive band logistics. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies, typically 2–5 messages per week. Details · Privacy · Terms.

The "Details", "Privacy" and "Terms" labels in the disclosure are inline links to this SMS Notifications page, the Privacy Policy, and the Terms of Service respectively.

How to opt out

Two equally valid ways:

  • Reply STOP (or CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, STOPALL) to any GrooveLinx SMS. You will receive one confirmation SMS and no further messages from us.
  • In the app: Sign in, open Settings → Notifications, scroll to SMS Notifications, and click the "✓ On" toggle to disable. Effect is immediate.

Help

Reply HELP or INFO to any GrooveLinx SMS to receive a help reply. Or email support@groovelinx.com.

Costs

GrooveLinx does not charge members for SMS notifications. Message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply.

Privacy and data handling

Your phone number is stored only in your own band's GrooveLinx account record. Mobile information and SMS opt-in consent data are not shared with third parties. No mobile information, phone numbers, or SMS opt-in consent data collected by GrooveLinx will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

Carrier liability

Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages. SMS delivery depends on your wireless carrier and on factors outside GrooveLinx's control.

GrooveLinx SMS Notifications

How members of bands using GrooveLinx opt in to and out of SMS notifications about their band's schedule.

What this is

GrooveLinx is a private band-management web application at groovelinx.com. Bands invite their own members; the app is not used for marketing or outreach to anyone outside an existing band. SMS is one of three notification channels (alongside in-app banners and browser push) that the app uses to notify members about activity in their own band — upcoming rehearsals and gigs, schedule changes, polls, and band-feed updates.

Who receives messages

Only people who are:

  1. Personally invited by their band leader to join GrooveLinx,
  2. Have created an account at groovelinx.com,
  3. Have signed in to that account, and
  4. Have explicitly enabled SMS notifications themselves in their account settings (the option is OFF by default).

Each band typically has 3–7 members. There is no marketing list, no purchased data, no third-party data sourcing.

How to opt in

  1. Sign in to your account at groovelinx.com.
  2. Open the menu and select Settings.
  3. Find the Notifications section.
  4. Enter your mobile phone number in the SMS field.
  5. Tap or click the Enable SMS notifications checkbox. (It is off by default — you must explicitly enable it.)
  6. Save your settings. You will receive a confirmation SMS.
Confirmation message you will receive:
"GrooveLinx: You've opted in to band notifications. Msg frequency varies, typically a few per week. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel. Msg & data rates may apply."

How to opt out

Two equally valid ways:

  • Reply STOP (or CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, STOPALL) to any GrooveLinx SMS. You will receive one confirmation SMS and no further messages from us.
  • In the app: Sign in, open Settings → Notifications, and uncheck "Enable SMS notifications." Effect is immediate.

Help

Reply HELP or INFO to any GrooveLinx SMS to receive a help reply. Or contact support@groovelinx.com.

Message frequency

Variable. Messages are only sent when there is real activity in your band — typically a few per week, slightly more during gig-prep weeks, sometimes none for stretches. There is no marketing component and no scheduled broadcast.

Costs

GrooveLinx does not charge members for SMS notifications. Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply.

Privacy and data handling

Your phone number is stored only in your own band's GrooveLinx account record. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than sending the band notifications you opted in to. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
